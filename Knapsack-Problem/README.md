# Algorithms-And-Data-Structures: Knapsack-Problem
</br>
ðŸŒ± Given n packages with corresponding payments P, weights W and also given a car with capacity C (mass of taken items cannot exceed C). The task is to pack a car optimally - such that the sum of profits associated with taken items is the maximum possible. 

</br> --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- </br>

## Additional informations:
- Maximum number of items: 15,
- The smallest element in P and W: 0,
- The largest element in P and W: 1000,
- The number of instances (cars to pack, the number of iterations of the main for loop): 500.

## Input
ðŸŒ± In next lines (four lines represent one car):
- A number of items n.
- Payments of items P.
- Weights of items W.
- The maximum mass of a car C.

## Output
ðŸŒ± In next lines:
</br>
*The optimal profit.*</br>
